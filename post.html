Cograbig Institute of Arts Celebrates the Class of 2024 Graduation Ceremony

Cograbig Institute of Arts Celebrates the Class of 2024 Graduation Ceremony. Cograbig Institute of Arts is thrilled to announce its 2024 Graduation Ceremony , marking another milestone in our journey of creativity, innovation, and excellence. Since its establishment in 2019 and official registration in 2020, Cograbig has been a powerhouse in beauty, fashion, arts, and entertainment, continuously shaping the future of the industry.

This year’s graduation ceremony is not just a celebration of academic achievements but also a testament to the dedication, passion, and resilience of our graduates. With Cograbig’s commitment to professionalism and hands-on learning, the graduates of 2024 are ready to take on the dynamic world of arts and entertainment with confidence and expertise.

A Ceremony of Inspiration and Excellence

The Cograbig Class of 2024 Graduation Ceremony was graced by some of the biggest names in the industry, offering graduates the motivation and insight needed to excel in their respective fields. This event did not only highlight the academic success of our students but also reaffirm Cograbig’s mission to nurture untapped talent, foster innovation, and set new standards in the creative sector. From fashion designers and makeup artists to filmmakers and performers, our graduates are proof that Cograbig is a place where dreams turn into reality.

A Legacy of Achievements & Creativity

As we celebrate our graduating class, we also reflect on the incredible journey of Cograbig over the years. Our institution has earned significant recognition, including:
✅ Winners of the Lesotho Fashion Week Wool & Mohair Competition (2020)
✅ Recognized among the best makeup artistry institutions in Lesotho (2022)
✅ Successful hosting of fashion shows, movies, and creative events
These achievements are a reflection of our commitment to quality education, industry excellence, and empowering creatives. While we honor the Class of 2024, we also look ahead to the future of Cograbig. With our expanding course offerings, industry collaborations, and cutting-edge creative programs, we remain dedicated to pushing boundaries and redefining arts education. To our graduates – this is just the beginning! Your journey in the arts and entertainment industry starts here, and we cannot wait to see the impact you will make. Congratulations, Class of 2024!

Runway Revolution Fashion Show: A Spectacle of Style and Creativity

Fashion lovers, get ready! The Runway Revolution Fashion Show is set to take center stage this June, bringing together visionary designers, emerging talent, and fashion-forward audiences for an unforgettable evening of style, art, and innovation.
As one of the most anticipated fashion events of the year, this show promises to push boundaries, challenge norms, and celebrate the evolution of fashion in a spectacular display of creativity.

This year’s theme, "Revolution in Motion," pays homage to the ever-changing landscape of fashion—where tradition meets modernity and where bold statements redefine what it means to dress with confidence. Expect to see a stunning lineup of avant-garde designs, timeless couture, and cutting-edge streetwear that reflect the dynamic pulse of today’s fashion industry. Each collection will tell a unique story, blending fabrics, textures, and colors to create a runway experience like no other.

Beyond the fashion, Runway Revolution is more than just a show—it’s a platform for designers to showcase their artistry and for fashion enthusiasts to witness the future of style. The event will feature renowned designers, up-and-coming brands, and creative masterminds who are shaping the fashion industry’s next big trends.
From intricate handcrafted garments to bold, futuristic designs, every look on the runway will be a masterpiece in its own right. The audience can also look forward to live performances, exclusive interviews, and networking opportunities with some of the biggest names in the industry. Whether you're an aspiring designer, a fashion influencer, or simply someone who appreciates the art of dressing, Runway Revolution is the place to be.

Join us this June as we celebrate the power of fashion to inspire, challenge, and transform. Stay tuned for more details on designers, ticket sales, and exclusive behind-the-scenes content!
