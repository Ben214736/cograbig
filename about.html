About Cograbig Institute of Arts

Our Story

Cograbig Institute of Arts was born out of a passion for creativity, fashion, beauty, and the arts. Established in 2019 and officially registered in 2020, the institute was founded with the vision of providing high-quality education and professional training for aspiring creatives. From the very beginning, our goal has been to empower and nurture artistic talent, offering students the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in the competitive world of fashion, beauty, and entertainment.

20+ Courses

Cograbig Institute fo Arts has rapidly grown to become a leading hub for creative education, already offering more than 20 specialized courses. Our diverse programs cover areas such as fashion design, makeup-artistry, graphics design, Music, Theatre, and more, ensuring that students receive comprehensive training in their chosen creative fields. With a strong emphasis on hands-on learning and industry relevance, our courses are designed to equip students with the practical skills and professional knowldge needed to thrive in the competitive world of arts and entertainment.

The Future of Cograbig

We believe in continuous growth and innovation, ensuring that we remain a pioneer in creative education. Cograbig has already established itself as a hub for artistic excellence, and we have big dreams for the future. We are working towards building strong partnerships with international institutions, brands, and industry leaders, providing students with internship and job placement opportunities worldwide. The journey has just begun, and the future of Cograbig is filled with endless possibilities. Join us as we continue to inspire, educate, and empower the next generation of creative professionals!

Why Choose Us?

Fashion is an industry that thrives on connections. Attending Cograbig Institute of Arts can provide you with valuable opportunities for networking and industry exposure. We have partnerships with top brands, designers, and fashion events, allowing students to intern, collaborate, and gain real-world experience that can be invaluable when seeking employment in the industry.

High Qualified Professor

We take pride in our team of highly qualified professors who bring years of expertise in their respective fields. Our instructors are not just educators; they are industry professionals with real-world experience in fashion, beauty, arts, and entertainment. With a hands-on approach to teaching, they ensure that students gain practical skills and in-depth knowledge needed to succeed in their careers. Through mentorship and personalized guidance, our professors inspire students to unleash their creativity and push boundaries in their artistic journey

Our Achievements

Since its establishment, Cograbig has achieved remarkable milestones that demonstrate its commitment to excellence. In 2020, we won the prestigious Lesotho Fashion Week Wool & Mohair Competition, a recognition of our outstanding work in fashion and design. By 2022, we were named one of the best Makeup Artistry institutions in Lesotho, further proving our dedication to artistic innovation and skill development. These achievements reflect our mission to nurture and produce top-tier talent in the world of fashion, beauty, and the arts.
